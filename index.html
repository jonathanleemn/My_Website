About Me

Interests

A big passion of mine is traveling to different parts of the world and learning new languages. I dedicate a large portion of my spare time to self improvement; whether that be weightlifting, practicing guitar or even brushing up on some coding.

Personal Values

"I believe in truth. But I'm also a big fan of justice."

-Clark Kent

Goals

My longterm goals are to build a career that I love and to live by an inner score card. This means doing the things that make me happy and fulfilled.

Background

I was born and raised in Minnesota to a couple of immigrants who were way in over their heads. Despite this, I was the first of my family to graduate college from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities with a degree in Computer Science.

Living in Spain

During the 2019-2020 school year, I got to live and teach English abroad in Spain. It was an unforgettable experience and a large inspiration for creating this website.

Accomplishments

At one point, graduating with a degree in Computer Science seemed impossible to me. Living abroad in Spain also seemed like a distant dream. But I'm proud to say that I've done both and am ready to tackle the next big project in my life.
